Tarot cards have a rich and complex history that dates back several centuries. The origin of tarot is believed to be in 14th-century Italy, where the cards were initially used for playing games. It wasn't until later that tarot cards started being associated with divination and esoteric practices. Each card's symbolism, imagery, and position within the spread are interpreted to gain insight into the past, present, or future circumstances, as well as to explore personal emotions and relationships.
