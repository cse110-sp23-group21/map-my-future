Molybdomancy

Solid Tin

Info

Molybdomancy is a divinatory practice characterized by the melting of tin or lead, followed by the pouring of the molten metal into water. The resulting solid shapes assumed by the metal are regarded as symbolic representations that are thought to provide insights into future events. This ancient tradition involves interpreting and discerning the patterns and forms exhibited by the cooled metal as a means of forecasting the future.
